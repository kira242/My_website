Gravity Rush 2

Gravity Rush 2 is an action-adventure video game developed by SIE Japan Studio and Project Siren. The game follows the story of Kat, a gravity-shifting heroine, as she explores the floating cities of Hekseville and Jirga Para Lhao. Kat gains the ability to manipulate gravity, allowing her to fly and walk on walls, as she unravels the mysteries surrounding a gravitational disturbance known as the "gravity storm." Throughout the game, players encounter various challenges, enemies, and puzzles, all while uncovering the secrets of Kat's past and the origin of the gravitational anomalies. The game received praise for its unique gameplay mechanics, art style, and engaging narrative.
