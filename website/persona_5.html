Persona 5

Persona 5 is a Japanese role-playing game (JRPG) that follows the story of a high school student known by his codename "Joker." The protagonist is falsely accused of assault and is transferred to Shujin Academy in Tokyo as part of his probation. As Joker, players navigate both the student life and an alternate supernatural dimension known as the Metaverse. In the Metaverse, Joker and his friends, who are known as the Phantom Thieves, use their Persona abilities to change the hearts of corrupt individuals by entering "Palaces" created from their distorted desires. Throughout the game, players balance school life, forming bonds with friends (Confidants), and combating supernatural threats in the Metaverse. The narrative explores themes of rebellion, justice, and the impact of one's actions on society. As the story unfolds, the Phantom Thieves uncover a larger conspiracy and face powerful adversaries. Persona 5 received acclaim for its stylish presentation, deep characters, engaging story, and the unique blend of social simulation and turn-based combat mechanics.
